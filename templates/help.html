Help & Support

Welcome to the Help Center

We're here to assist you with any questions or issues you might have while using Internship Finder. Browse through our FAQs, guides, and resources to get the help you need.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

To create an account, click on the "Sign Up" button on the top right corner of the page. Fill in your details and submit the form. You will receive a confirmation email to verify your account.

How can I apply for internships?

After logging in, go to the "Internships" section. Browse the available listings and click on the ones you're interested in. Follow the application instructions provided in each listing.

What should I do if I forget my password?

If you've forgotten your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Follow the instructions to reset your password using your email address.

How do I update my profile?

Go to the "Settings" page from your dashboard. Here, you can update your personal information, change your password, and adjust your preferences.

How can I change my email address?

To change your email address, visit the "Settings" page and update your email information. You may need to verify the new email address.

How can I report an issue with an internship listing?

If you find an issue with an internship listing, please use the "Report an Issue" button on the listing page or contact our support team directly.

Can I apply for multiple internships at once?

Yes, you can apply for multiple internships. Ensure that you meet the requirements for each listing and follow the application instructions provided.

How can I contact the company offering an internship?

Contact details for each company are usually provided in the internship listing. If not, you can use the contact form on their website or reach out via their provided email.

How can I provide feedback about the platform?

You can provide feedback using the "Give Us Feedback" section on this page. We value your input and use it to improve our services.

What should I do if I suspect fraudulent activity?

If you suspect fraudulent activity, please report it immediately using the "Report an Issue" feature or contact our support team.

What if I experience technical issues?

If you experience technical issues, please clear your browser cache and cookies. If the issue persists, contact our support team for assistance.

Can I delete my account?

Yes, you can delete your account from the "Settings" page. Please note that this action is permanent and will remove all your data from our system.

Are my data and privacy secure?

We take your privacy seriously and implement strong security measures to protect your data. For more information, please review our privacy policy.

Can I change the language of the platform?

The platform currently supports English. We are working on adding support for more languages in the future.

How do I find internships in a specific location?

Use the location filter in the "Internships" section to search for opportunities based on your preferred location.
